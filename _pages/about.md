I am an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Fluid Science, Tohoku University. My research focuses on charged gas–liquid interfaces, cavitation bubble dynamics, plasma–liquid interactions, high-voltage pulsed discharges, and non-equilibrium transport phenomena.

My current research investigates how electrical charge, external electric fields, phase change, and interfacial dynamics can be coupled to control cavitation bubbles and transient gas–liquid systems.
